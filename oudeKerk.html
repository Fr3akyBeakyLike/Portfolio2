Oude Kerk AR

I chose to do a minor about VR. This minor challenged me to work in a team and create a product for a client. There were multiple teams and we had to pitch our ideas to different companies, the Old Church of Amsterdam really wanted to work with us.

The challenge we got from them was as followed: How do we connect the present with the past using the buried people and the contemporary art in the church?

We solved the problem with an AR (augmented reality) experience using Vuforia in Unity for our app. And Unity in general for our Hololens experience.
