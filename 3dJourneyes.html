3D Journeys

This was a small challenge which I got at school. We had to make a seamless multi-device experience, made possible by atleast three API's.

With 3D journeys you as a traveler will be able to share your journey with family and friends. All the pictures and notes you take while travelling will be saved. Once you come back you can relive your travel experience in Virtual Reality or Google Street View.
