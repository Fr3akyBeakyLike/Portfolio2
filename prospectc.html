Prospect C

A service made for creative producers and creative specialists in the theatre industry. This service helps the creative producer create a team for any assigned project. With her own database within the service of ProsPect C she can find specialists she worked with before and add them again to a new project.

Here she can play with the given budget, and try out different teams. When the final team has been settled, they can exchange files with each other needed for the production.
